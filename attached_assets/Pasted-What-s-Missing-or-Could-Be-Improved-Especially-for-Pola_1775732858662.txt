What's Missing or Could Be Improved (Especially for Polar Plunges / Group Events)This view works for very basic free meetups, but for real-world cold plunge events (including paid ones or charity-style polar plunges), several practical gaps stand out:No Cover Image / Visual Header
You mentioned this is planned for 2.0 with server image storage — that's fine for now. Without it, the page looks a bit plain (just the calendar icon). Once added, it will make events much more appealing in lists and details.
Safety / Waiver Acknowledgment (Biggest gap for liability)
Polar plunges and group cold events almost always require a waiver or risk acknowledgment. Real events (charity or otherwise) typically make participants sign (or check) a liability release covering hypothermia, cold shock, injury, etc. Many require it before participation, sometimes with parental consent for minors.
Recommendation: Add a required step on the Sign Up flow (not just creation):  Checkbox: "I acknowledge the risks of cold water plunging and agree to the event safety guidelines and liability waiver."  
Link to view full text (or PDF upload by organizer).
Tie it to your app-wide Safety Agreement for consistency. This protects organizers and makes the feature feel professional/safer than Facebook Events.
Payment / Fee Handling
The "$20 event fee" mention in the description box is good, but there's no built-in way to collect or confirm payment during Sign Up.
Most organized polar plunges and studio events do collect fees — flat entry ($15–$50 common), suggested donations, or minimum fundraising pledges for charity versions.
Quick win: On the Sign Up screen (after tapping the big button), add an optional or required "Payment Link" confirmation, or at least a note: "Pay the $20 fee via [external link provided by organizer] before attending."
For now, let organizers paste the link in the description or contact section. Full in-app payments can come later.
More "What to Expect" Details
The current box is solid but could be expanded slightly for better attendee prep:  Expected water temperature (if provided during creation).  
Safety briefing time or "never plunge alone" reminder.  
Post-plunge plans (e.g., "Hot drinks and chairs provided" or "Nearby sauna option").  
Difficulty level or beginner-friendly note.
Notifications & Reminders
No mention of automatic reminders (e.g., "1 day before" or "1 hour before" push). These are very useful for outdoor events where no-shows happen.
Post-Event Engagement (Nice-to-have for retention)
After the event date passes, add options like:  "I Attended – Log My Plunge" (auto-adds to Cold Score/streak/history).  
Upload group photo or mark attendance.
This turns one-off events into ongoing app usage.
