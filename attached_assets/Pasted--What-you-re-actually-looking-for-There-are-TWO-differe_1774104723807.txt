🔑 What you’re actually looking for

There are TWO different “3rd party HR” paths in the Zepp ecosystem:

1. 📡 Bluetooth HR Broadcast (REAL-TIME)

👉 This is what you want for apps/dev integration

2. 🔗 App permission sharing (Health Connect / Apple Health)

👉 This is delayed + not useful for live app integration

✅ OPTION A — Real-time HR broadcast (the one you want)
On the watch:
Open Workout
Pick any workout (you don’t have to start it yet)
Scroll settings / options
Look for:
“Heart Rate Broadcast”
or “HR Sharing”
Turn it ON

👉 Once enabled:

The watch starts broadcasting HR over BLE
Uses standard Bluetooth Heart Rate Profile (HRP)
Your app can connect like it would to a chest strap
⚠️ Important quirks (this trips people up)
It’s often ONLY active during workouts
It may:
stop when workout ends
require re-enabling
Not visible in normal settings menus

From real-world users:

“Amazfit has a built-in heart rate share app, just turn it on”

❌ OPTION B — Zepp “3rd party access” (NOT what you want)

Path:

Zepp app → Profile → 3rd-party account linking

This includes:

Health Connect
Google Fit
Strava

You can enable heart rate permissions there:

“Profile > 3rd-party account linking > Health Connect… allow heart rate”

BUT:

❌ Not real-time
❌ Not accessible directly by your app
❌ Cloud-mediated
🧠 Key takeaway (for your cold plunge app)

If your goal is:

“pull HR into my app automatically”

You want:
Bluetooth HR broadcast mode
NOT Zepp integrations
🛠️ Dev-level detail (useful for you)

Once broadcast is ON:

Device appears like:
Amazfit T-Rex 2
or generic HR device
Uses:
Standard BLE Heart Rate Service (0x180D)
Characteristic:
0x2A37 (Heart Rate Measurement)

👉 This is HUGE because:

No reverse engineering needed
Works with:
iOS CoreBluetooth
Android BLE
any fitness API
⚠️ One limitation (important for your app design)

You cannot pull HR in the background continuously unless:

workout is active
or broadcast is manually enabled

So UX-wise, you may need:

“Start session” → triggers HR broadcast usage

🏁 Bottom line
There’s no obvious “3rd party HR” toggle in Zepp settings
The feature is:
👉 hidden inside workout → HR broadcast
Uses standard BLE HR profile (easy to integrate once enabled)
