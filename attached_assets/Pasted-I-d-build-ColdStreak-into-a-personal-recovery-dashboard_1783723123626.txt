The biggest feature I would build: "ColdStreak Intelligence"

Instead of graphs...

Give users insights.

Examples:

"You lose weight fastest when plunging before breakfast."
"Your average heart-rate recovery has improved 18% in the past month."
"You maintain longer streaks when plunging on weekdays."
"Your mood improves most after 4-minute plunges."
"You sleep best after evening contrast therapy."
"Your average plunge temperature has dropped 6°F since joining."

These make the app feel like a coach, not just a logbook.

Community comparisons

Without exposing private data, let users opt in to anonymous benchmarking:

You're in the top 12% for consistency.
Your average plunge time is 5.8 minutes (community average: 3.9).
People your age typically plunge at 46°F; you average 39°F.
Users with 100+ plunges improve heart-rate recovery by an average of X%.

Gamification like this encourages engagement while preserving privacy.

If I had to prioritize the roadmap
Health integrations (Apple Health + Health Connect) so users can automatically bring in weight, body fat, heart rate, sleep, and related metrics.
Progress dashboard with trends and charts.
AI-generated weekly insights that explain what the data means.
Recovery Score and personalized plunge recommendations.
Community benchmarking and opt-in comparisons.
